Voikeo Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Voikeo ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the Voikeo mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

When you use Voikeo, we may collect the following information:

  • Account information — your name, email address, and profile photo, collected when you sign in with Google.
  • Voice recordings — audio files you record and choose to save or share within the app.
  • User-generated content — titles, tags, and comments you create within the app.
  • Usage data — information about how you interact with the app, such as recordings played, liked, or bookmarked.
  • Device information — basic device data required for the app to function correctly.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your Voikeo account
  • Store and deliver your voice recordings
  • Enable social features such as likes, comments, bookmarks, and following
  • Improve and develop the Voikeo app
  • Communicate with you about your account or app updates
  • Process premium subscription payments (Voikeo+)

3. Third-Party Services

Voikeo uses the following third-party services to operate:

  • Google Firebase — for authentication, database storage, and file storage. Firebase is subject to Google's Privacy Policy at policies.google.com/privacy.
  • Google Sign-In — for account creation and login.
  • RevenueCat (planned) — for managing Voikeo+ premium subscriptions.
  • Google Play In-App Payments — for processing subscription payments on Android.

We do not sell your personal information to any third party.

4. Microphone Access

Voikeo requires access to your device microphone to enable voice recording, which is the core feature of the app. Audio is only recorded when you explicitly tap the record button. We do not record audio in the background.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase infrastructure. Voice recordings are stored in Firebase Storage and recording metadata is stored in Firebase Firestore. We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data, but no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

6. Public and Private Content

When you save a recording as Public, it may be visible to other Voikeo users in the Discover feed. When you save a recording as Private, it is only accessible to you in your Vault. You can change the visibility of your recordings at any time.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your personal data and recordings will be permanently deleted from our systems within 30 days.

8. Children's Privacy

Voikeo is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Export your data

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at voikeoss@gmail.com.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page. Your continued use of Voikeo after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
